## Statement of Work — Standard Structure

When generating SOWs, follow this structure:

1. **SOW Reference** — `SOW-STELLAR-[###]`, Date, Parties, Phase name
2. **Background** — one paragraph on Project Stellar purpose and context
3. **Scope of Work** — In Scope (ref table), Out of Scope (ref table)
4. **Deliverables** — numbered, tied to phase milestones
5. **Timeline** — phase duration, key milestones, assumed start date
6. **Assumptions** — client-side dependencies (API access, stakeholder availability, timely sign-off)
7. **Fees** — hourly rate + GST, estimated hours, billing cadence (fortnightly)
8. **Acceptance criteria** — written sign-off or deemed accepted within 5 business days
9. **Authorisation** — signature block (Ben du Chateau / Stellar authorised signatory)

---

## Out of Scope (standard exclusions for all SOWs)

Always include unless explicitly agreed otherwise:
- Immigration or visa advisory functionality
- Payroll or contractor payment processing
- Native mobile applications (web-based only)
- Data migration from legacy systems beyond JobAdder
- Third-party security penetration testing
- Ongoing hosting and third-party subscription costs (borne by Client)
- Anything not explicitly listed as in scope
